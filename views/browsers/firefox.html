how to enable 3rd party cookies for
the domain <%= domain %> in the browser firefox

  1. Click the Hamburger button in the top right of your browser window.

  2. Click the Preferences option in the menu that pops up

  3. In the new window that pops up, click the Privacy tab

  4. In the Privacy tab, click the Exceptions button.

    If you don't see it, 3rd party cookies should be enabled already.

  5. Type <%= domain %> into the Address of website field and click Allow.

  6. Your changes should now be saved.

  7. Close the settings window and return to what you were doing!
