Enabled 3rd party cookies for <%= domain %> in Chrome

  1. Open a new window and type chrome://settings into the URL bar.

  2. You should now be on the Chrome Settings page.

  3. Find the Search box in the top right corner.

  4. Search for Content Settings in the search box.

  5. Click the Content Settings button.

  6. Click the Manage Excpetions button.

  7. Type [*.]<%= domain %> into the left field and select Allow on the right.

  8. Hit the Enter button and it should submit your changes.

  9. Close the settings window and return to what you were doing!
